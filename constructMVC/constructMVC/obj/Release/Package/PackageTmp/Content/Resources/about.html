About Us

EECS is a client-focused, service-oriented firm engaged in wide variety of projects from General construction to I.T. facilities development. We bridge the gap between the integration of traditional general construction services and advance structures like data-enabled facilities.

Initially started as subcontractor for wide variety of projects from large companies that helped us developed knowledge-driven solutions for complex construction projects. Currently, our expertise spans from general masonry works, house/building renovations to IT facilities construction.
